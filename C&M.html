Can I save a draft of my form before submission?
  • Yes, You can save it and work on it later.
How can I save my records in draft and work on it later ?
  • After creating a form, click on Save Incomplete NCR.
  • Go to NCR Storage and find the record. There is an option of edit and delete by which you can work on your incomplete files.
Can i get notification from this website
  • Yes, You can get notification.
  • Go to Engineering Page and mark 'Yes' on the question where they are asking for any notification from the site.
