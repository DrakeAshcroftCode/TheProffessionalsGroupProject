Do I need to Login/Register?
  • Yes, You have to login/register to use our services and to make a record book.
How can I create a NCR form?
  • Firstly, Login to our website then click on Create NCR form.
  • It will automatically save your record.
Where do I find my records?
  • Go to NCR storage.
  • Search your record by Year, 3 digit #extension and Supplier Name.
  • Make sure the details are correct.
How can I edit or delete a form after submission?
  • Go to NCR storage, fill the details and click search.
  • In the records list, you will see the options for edit and delete.
Is there any limit on numbers of form that I can make?
  • No, You can create as more as you want.
Is my data safe and secure?
Only authorized personnel have access to the data, and we use strict authentication protocols to prevent unauthorized access.
